About Me

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Science and Technology Beijing, School of Computer and Communication. My current research interest lies in chart understanding, with a particular focus on extracting and interpreting structured information from scientific figures.

I am broadly interested in computer vision, document understanding, and visual information extraction. I enjoy building practical systems that connect visual perception with structured reasoning, especially for complex visual content such as charts, diagrams, and technical documents.
